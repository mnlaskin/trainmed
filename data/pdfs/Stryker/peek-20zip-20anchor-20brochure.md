PEEK Zip Anchor
The PEEK Zip Anchor is fully cannulated and features a trocar-tip inserter to distribute 
insertion torque along the length of the implant. No tap is needed. The cannulation of the 
anchor provides a direct channel from the bone marrow to the rotator cuff.
The PEEK Zip Anchor is designed with independent suture eyelets, which are designed to 
separate each strand of the Force Fiber working suture for easier suture management.
Available in 5.5mm and 6.5mm sizes, with and without half-circle taper needles, the PEEK 
Zip Anchor is a chosen anchor for rotator cuff and other soft-tissue repairs.
