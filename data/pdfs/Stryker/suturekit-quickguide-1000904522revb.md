# Suture Kit Quick Guide (XBraid TT soft-tissue repair kits)

XBraid TT is a smooth, non-absorbable low 
profile, 100% ultra-high molecular weight 
polyethylene suture tape designed to lie flat 
against the tissue and increase tendon-to-bone 
contact in soft tissue repairs.1 
NiceLoop is a non-absorbable braided suture for 
reliable fixation. It comes pre-looped, sterile and 
on a needle with a self-stabilized NiceKnot that 
prevents slippage.2
