XBraid TT is a smooth, low profile, 100% ultra-high molecular weight 
polyethylene suture tape designed to increase tendon-to-bone contact in 
soft tissue repairs. The custom design of the XBraidTT allows the suture 
to lie flat against the tissue to increase the suture footprint.
XBraid TT
Features and benefits
• XBraid TT is a tie-able tape with comparable knot stacks to #2 suture.
• Designed with lubricity in mind, XBraid TT easily slides after anchor insertion and during knot tying.
• Easy to open packaging designed to help with efficiency in the operating room.
• Custom braid technology provides a suture tape that is specifically designed to not fold under tension.
• Ultimate tissue pull through load: 206N2

Force Fiber is a coreless, surgical suture designed with a low knot profile 
and better knot security than comparative sutures with a core. Unlike polyblend 
and some polyester sutures, Force Fiber is composed of a proprietary combination of 
materials that allow for a unique braid construction, designed to contribute to its strength. 
It is a non-absorbable suture composed of ultra-high molecular weight, polyethylene fiber 
allowing for a durable and strong solution.

Features and benefits
• 53% better tensile strength than the leading competitor.1*
