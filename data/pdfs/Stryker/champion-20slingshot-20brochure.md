# Champion Slingshot Capsule Restoration — Brochure

SUTURE PASSERSSlingShot

EFFICIENT

The Champion Slingshot has 
been optimized for use in the shoulder 
and allows the surgeon to both pass and 
retrieve suture using a single portal. Its 
ergonomic thumb-slide design helps to 
eliminate additional procedural steps 
and potentially reduce operating time. 
EFFICIENT

Optimized for the Shoulder
Ergonomically designed thumb-slide mechanism helps to simplify the suture passing and retrieval steps.
The small Nitinol Arms allow for the ability to quickly retrieve the suture, while the razor sharp needle tip 
helps to minimize potential damage to the labral tissue.

Versatility
With a 45° Left, 45° Right, and 70° Up tip option, the Champion SlingShot is specifically designed to 
address various labral pathologies of the Shoulder.
