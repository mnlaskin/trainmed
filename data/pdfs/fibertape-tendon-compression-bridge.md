Product Overview
The FiberTape Tendon Compression Bridge Kit provides a simple and efficient way to repair the subscapularis 
tendon after a total shoulder arthroplasty or hemiarthroplasty. This technique can be used when either a 
subscapularis peel or lesser tuberosity osteotomy (LTO) is completed.
